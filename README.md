### Introduction

ExpenseTrack+, a comprehensive daily expense tracker. ExpenseTrack+ will enable users to efficiently manage their finances by tracking their income, expenses, and generating detailed monthly reports. This project aims to provide users with a user-friendly platform to monitor their spending habits and make informed financial decisions.
